BRANDING AND LOGO

Agrilution


Agrilution combines the project „Gardening will Save the World“ by designer Tom Dixion in collaboration with the furniture store Ikea and the Munich-based company of the same name. The company makes use of the technological concept of „Gardening will Save the World“ and combines it with the Munich company‘s Plantcube.
Greenery is planted in the basement with the help of hydroponics technology, where the growth rate is supported by LED lighting. Vegetables, herbs and salad leaves are grown vertically around a central column, with nutrient-rich water sprayed directly onto the roots. On the roof, traditional cultivation methods are used, giving the city a generally greener and more natural aesthetic.

CHRONOLOGY
Agrilution is a company that is placed in the time of cyberpunk, but a turnaround and reorientation is spreading among the people. The ever-increasing discontent of the population is forcing the monopolies of power to make drastic changes. The goal is to build an environment like that of the solarpunk in order to improve both the quality of life for the people and the environment. Agrilution is now helping people to achieve this goal, using the most advanced technological resources and making them accessible to everyone.
