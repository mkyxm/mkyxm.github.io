APP DESIGN

LIV-BAYERN

Our task was to develop an app for the Landesinnungsverband der Zimmerer Bayern (LIV) as part of the Interface Design II module. Mr. Kai Gajewski from the LIV makes the first important (customer) wishes clear, which will accompany us throughout the entire conception, design and realization process. The structure of the app to be developed should be based on the existing public website of the LIV and its content in combination with the internal, password-protected part of the site should be transferred into a coherent, user-oriented app design.

»WIR BAUEN EINE […] [APP], WIE SIE EUCH GEFÄLLT.«

The challenge for us is to develop an app with the characteristics of overview, completeness, personalization and flexibility, which at the same time visualizes the goals and values of the LIV of Carpenters Bavaria, for a multi-layered target group against the background of the customer briefing and the high complexity of the website.
