MIKE MEIRÉ

An artist, designer, art director, and curator, Mike Meiré is considered a leading protagonist of cutting-edge editorial design, he has worked for various magazines, including brand eins, Arch+, 032c, Neue Zürcher Zeitung, Interview Magazine and GQ Italia. Together with his brother he has been running an agency Meiré und Meiré since 1987.
