SEA YOU FESTIVAL

Sea You is one of the most beautiful beach festivals in Germany. Located at the Tunisee in Freiburg, a unique festival landscape awaits the visitors with the "Beach Republic". The line-up usually features the greats of electronic music - from classic to progressive.

REDESIGN

With the brand refresh, the Sea You Festival is repositioning itself in terms of a holistic brand profiling. The extraordinary profile is not only visible in the communication - but the unique "Beach Republic Feeling" is also medially transported and experienced with the new, activating visual design. At the same time, the appearance full of character creates a strong and independent positioning in the grown festival landscape.
